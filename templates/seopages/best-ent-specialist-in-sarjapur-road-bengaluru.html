General ENT Consultation

Thorough evaluation of ear, nose, and throat health to address common yet persistent symptoms like chronic cough, sore throat, or ear fullness.

Sinus & Allergy Management

Specialized care for chronic sinusitis, nasal polyps, and allergic rhinitis, focusing on identifying triggers and providing long-term relief.

Expert management of middle ear infections, wax impaction, and tinnitus. We provide precise hearing tests to monitor auditory health across all ages.
