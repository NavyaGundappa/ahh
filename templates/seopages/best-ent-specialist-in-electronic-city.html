Comprehensive ENT & Hearing Care Services

​We provide a wide spectrum of treatments addressing everything from common seasonal allergies to complex structural issues of the head and neck.

General ENT Consultation :

​A thorough evaluation of the ear, nose, and throat to address persistent coughs, throat pain, or congestion. Early intervention at our clinic prevents minor irritations from turning into chronic conditions.

​Sinus & Allergy Management :

​Living in Bengaluru often means dealing with dust and pollen triggers. We offer specialized care for chronic sinusitis, nasal polyps, and allergic rhinitis, focusing on long-term relief rather than just temporary fixes.

​​Ear Infections & Audiometry:

​As a leading ENT doctor near Electronic City, we treat middle ear infections, glue ear, and tinnitus. We also provide comprehensive hearing tests to evaluate auditory health for all age groups.

​Pediatric ENT Care:

​Children require a gentler approach. We specialize in treating tonsillitis, adenoid issues, and ear-fluid buildup in children, ensuring a stress-free experience for both the child and the parents.

​Surgical & Minor Procedures:

​For conditions that require more than medication, we perform specialized procedures including septoplasty (for deviated septum), tonsillectomy, and micro-ear surgeries.

​What to expect on your first visit:

Your initial consultation will involve a detailed medical history review, a physical examination using specialized ENT tools, and, if required, on-the-spot diagnostic tests to get to the root of the problem immediately.
