​Our General Surgery Services & Treatments Offered

​Comprehensive General Surgical Care

​The general surgery department at Aarogya Hastha manages diverse pathologies through both open and minimally invasive approaches:

​Diagnostic Evaluation:

Thorough clinical assessments and surgical planning for abdominal pain, swellings, and internal disorders.

Laparoscopic (Minimally Invasive) Surgery:

Utilizing small-port access for procedures to reduce recovery time and post-operative discomfort.

​Appendix Surgery (Appendectomy):

Prompt surgical removal of the appendix to treat acute or chronic appendicitis.

Hernia Repair Protocols:

Specialized management of inguinal, umbilical, and incisional hernias through tension-free mesh techniques.

Gallbladder Surgery (Cholecystectomy):

Efficient removal of the gallbladder to resolve symptomatic gallstones or inflammation.

​Proctology Treatments:

Advanced clinical management for anorectal conditions including piles (hemorrhoids), fissures, and fistulas.

Breast Health Services:

Evaluation of breast lumps, biopsies, and minor corrective breast surgeries.

​Endocrine Surgery:

Specialized operative care for thyroid nodules and other glandular swellings.

Minor Procedures & Trauma Care:

Management of abscesses, lipomas, cyst removals, and comprehensive wound care for complex injuries.
