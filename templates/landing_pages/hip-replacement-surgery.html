Is This You?

Don't Let Hip Pain Hold You Back

If any of these sounds familiar, it's time to talk to an orthopaedic specialist about a Permanent solution.

Constant Hip or Groin Pain

Deep aching in the hip, groin, or thigh that worsens with activity. Pain when getting out of a car, climbing stairs, or simply standing up from a chair.

Pain That Keeps You Awake

Sleeping on the affected side is impossible. You wake up multiple times at night. Painkillers barely take the edge off, and the relief doesn't last.

Walking Has Become a Struggle

You limp, you lean, you avoid walks. Putting on shoes or socks is a daily battle. You've stopped doing things you love because your hip aren't cooperate.

Self-Assessment

Do You Need a Hip Replacement?

Tap each symptom that applies to you. This is a starting point — not a diagnosis.

  • Hip or groin pain that limits walking or standing
  • Stiffness that makes bending or squatting difficult
  • Pain even at rest or during sleep
  • Painkillers or injections no longer provide relief
  • X-ray shows arthritis or avascular necrosis
  • You've had a hip fracture or previous injury
  • Daily activities (dressing, bathing, walking) are affected
