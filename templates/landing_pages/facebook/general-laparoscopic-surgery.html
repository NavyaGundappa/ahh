What We Treat

Laparoscopy & General Surgery Procedures

From routine gallbladder removal to complex hernia repair — our surgeons use the latest minimally invasive techniques for faster healing.

Most Common

Gallbladder Removal (Cholecystectomy)

Laparoscopy removal of gallbladder for gallstones. 3 tiny keyhole incision, same-day or next-day discharge. No large scars.

Hernia Repair (Lap / Open)

Inguinal, umbilical, incisional, Ventral and hiatal hernia repair using mesh reinforcement. Laparoscopy option for faster recovery.

Appendix Removal (Appendectomy)

Emergency and elective Laparoscopy appendectomy. Minimal pain, small incisions, back to work in a week.

Piles, Fissure & Fistula

Laser and minimally invasive procedures for haemorrhoids, fissures, and fistula. Reduced pain, faster healing.

Thyroid Surgery (Thyroidectomy)

Safe removal of thyroid gland for nodules, goitre, or cancer. Expert endocrine surgeons with minimal scarring.

Why Laparoscopy?

Keyhole Surgery vs. Traditional Open Surgery

See why patients — and surgeons — prefer the minimally invasive approach.

Smaller Incisions

3-4 tiny cuts (5-10mm) vs one large cut (15-20cm)

Less Pain

Significantly less post-operative pain and discomfort

Faster Discharge

Faster discharge than open surgery, shorter hospital stay

Quick Recovery

Back to normal activities in 1-2 weeks vs 4-6 weeks
