Empowering your journey towards better health

Good doctors know their medicine, but great doctors know their patients

With the combination of clinical values, Aarogya Hastha’s department of Internal Medicine strives to provide excellence that is proven and age-old, with the right diagnosis and expertise. Thus ensuring a holistic approach to patient care.

Services

The Department of Internal Medicine at Aarogya Hasta Hospital provides a comprehensive range of healthcare services focused on the prevention, diagnosis, and treatment of adult medical conditions. Their offerings include:

Primary Care

The department serves its patients as the first point of contact for seeking medical attention. Conduct routine check-ups, manage chronic illnesses and address general health concerns.

Specialised Consultations

Experienced internists offer expert consultations in various subspecialities

Diagnostic Services

We are equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic facilities, including radiology (X-rays, CT Scans) and laboratory services. This ensures an accurate and timely assessment of a patient's condition.

Chronic Disease Management

Assist patients in managing chronic conditions, including diabetes, hypertension, heart disease and respiratory disorders. Provide personalised treatment plans and monitor progress.

Infectious Disease Care

Diagnosis and treatment of a wide range of infectious diseases, offering expertise in areas like HIV/AIDS management, antibiotic stewardship and immunisation recommendations.

Preventive Care

Offer preventive measures to maintain overall health. This includes vaccinations, lifestyle counselling and screenings for conditions like cancer, hypertension and diabetes.

Geriatric Care

Specialised care for elderly patients, focusing on age-related health concerns, geriatric syndromes and maintaining quality of life.

Acute Illness Management

Handle acute medical conditions, including respiratory infections, gastrointestinal issues and various types of fevers.

Hospital Inpatient Care

Oversee the care of hospitalised patients, coordinating treatment plans and managing complex medical conditions.

Intensive Care

In collaboration with critical care specialists, provide advanced care for critically ill patients in the Intensive Care Unit (ICU).

Pain Management

Offer solutions for chronic pain through a multidisciplinary approach, combining medical, physical and psychological interventions.

Postoperative Care

Work closely with surgical teams to provide comprehensive care for patients before and after surgery, ensuring a smooth recovery process.

Collaborative Approach

Fosters a multidisciplinary approach, working closely with other specialities for its patients to provide comprehensive care to treat complex medical conditions.

Patient Education

Educate patients about their conditions, treatment options and lifestyle modifications to promote self-care and overall well-being.

Aarogya Hastha is committed to delivering high-quality, patient-centred care, emphasising both preventive measures and advanced treatment options for a wide range of medical conditions.
