Types of Anaesthesia Care

At Aarogya Hastha, we understand the importance of general anaesthesia and its effects on a patient's health and safety. We provide advanced general anaesthesia services to ensure our patients' most comfortable and safe experience.

Regional

  • Spinal
  • Epidural anaesthesia and analgesia
  • Peripheral nerve block

Regional anaesthesia is a type of anaesthesia that numbs a specific body part where surgery or a procedure is to be performed. This type of anaesthesia can be delivered in different forms, such as spinal, epidural, or peripheral nerve blocks.

Daycare Surgery

Aarogya Hastha offers Total Intravenous Anaesthesia (TIVA) to patients requiring daycare surgery. With this advanced technique, many surgeries can be performed and the patient can be discharged on the same day with minimal risk of adverse events.
