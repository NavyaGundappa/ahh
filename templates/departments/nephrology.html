Nephrology

Strengthening the foundation of your health: your kidneys

Welcome to Aarogya Hastha Hospital's Nephrology department, where we offer top-quality care for all types of kidney diseases, disorders, and complications. Our team of dedicated nephrologists, nurses and support staff work together to provide personalized treatment and care plans to help our patients achieve optimal kidney health.

Our Services:

Diagnosis and Treatment of Kidney Diseases
Our department is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic technology, including ultrasound, MRI, and CT scans, to diagnose any type of kidney disease accurately. Our team of experienced nephrologists then work closely with patients to create personalized treatment plans, which may include medication, specialized diets, and other therapies.
Hemodialysis and Peritoneal Dialysis
We offer in-centre hemodialysis and at-home peritoneal dialysis services for patients with chronic kidney disease. Our dialysis units are fully equipped with modern machines and trained staff to provide safe and efficient dialysis treatments.
Kidney Transplant
Our nephrology department has a successful kidney transplant program for patients whose kidneys have failed and need a transplant as a treatment option. Our team of experienced transplant surgeons perform both live donor as well as deceased donor transplants, providing comprehensive pre-and post-operative care.
Hypertension Management
High blood pressure is one of the leading causes of kidney disease. Our nephrologists specialize in managing and treating this condition, helping patients to maintain healthy blood pressure levels and prevent kidney damage.
Diabetic Kidney Disease Management
Diabetes is another common cause of kidney disease. Our nephrology team works closely with our endocrinologists and other diabetes specialists to help patients manage their blood sugar levels and prevent or slow down the progression of diabetic kidney disease.
Comprehensive Kidney Stone Treatment
Our nephrologists work closely with our urologists to provide a comprehensive treatment plan for patients with kidney stones, including medication, diet changes, and minimally invasive procedures such as lithotripsy.

At Aarogya Hastha Hospital, we also prioritize education and prevention. Our department also has specialized facilities for specific kidney conditions, such as glomerulonephritis, polycystic kidney disease, and inherited kidney disorders, to provide targeted and comprehensive care for our patients.
