Orthopaedics

Regain mobility, live pain-free lives

Aarogya Hastha can provide quality care for patients dealing with musculoskeletal disorders, injuries and diseases. From joint replacements and sports injuries to spinal conditions and paediatric orthopaedics, we offer a complete list of services to help our patients regain mobility and improve their quality of life.

Our Services

Trauma

We specialise in the treatment of traumas, including fractures, dislocations, sprains and strains. Trauma patients receive specialised care from a multidisciplinary team of orthopaedic surgeons, physiotherapists and occupational therapists to ensure an efficient diagnosis with a comprehensive treatment plan. From minor injuries to complex cases, our team is dedicated to help patients regain mobility and overcome traumas.

Arthroplasty

Arthroplasty involves reconstructing or replacing a damaged joint, such as the hip, knee and shoulder. This procedure aims to relieve pain and improve joint function, thus enhancing patients’ overall quality of life. We have the latest technology and techniques that ensure successful outcomes for arthroplasty procedures. From diagnosis to post-surgery rehabilitation, our orthopaedic department provides comprehensive care to patients, helping them regain mobility and live pain-free lives.

Arthroscopy and Sports Medicine

Arthroscopy is a minimally invasive procedural technique to diagnose and treat problems surgically within a joint. This technique is frequently used in sports medicine, which specialises in treating and preventing injuries. We work closely to treat injuries, provide preventative care and help them achieve optimal performance.

Spine Surgery

Spine surgery is a specialised procedure that focuses on treating conditions and injuries related to the spine. Orthopaedics department at Aarogya Hastha have a team of skilled and experienced surgeons dedicated to provide the best care. Our advanced surgical techniques allow us to effectively diagnose and treat various spinal conditions, including degenerative diseases, tumours and traumatic injuries.
