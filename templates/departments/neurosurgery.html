Neurosurgery

Welcome to our Neurosurgery department at Aarogya Hastha Hospital, the prime destination for all your neurological needs. With the commitment to providing the highest level of care through our dedicated team of neurosurgeons and medical professionals, we offer treatment for all neurological disorders. At Aarogya Hastha Hospital, our state-of-the-art medical infrastructure is equipped with the latest technology and equipment to provide complete care for a wide range of neurological conditions.

Our Services:

Brain Tumor Surgery
Aarogya Hastha Hospital offers advanced surgical procedures for the treatment of brain tumours. Our highly skilled neurosurgeons use minimally invasive techniques to remove tumours while preserving neurological function and minimizing complications.
Spinal Surgery
We specialize in diagnosing and treating spinal disorders such as herniated discs, spinal stenosis, and spinal cord injuries. Our surgeons are trained and offer the latest techniques to provide the best possible outcomes for our patients.
Head Trauma & Neurotrauma Care
Our neurosurgery team is well-equipped to treat patients with brain and spinal injuries caused by accidents, falls, or other trauma. We provide immediate and advanced care to prevent further damage and promote healing.
Pediatric Neurosurgery
We understand the unique needs of our youngest patients and provide specialized care for neurological conditions affecting children. Our expert team is experienced in treating conditions such as brain and spinal cord malformations, hydrocephalus, and pediatric brain tumours.
Peripheral Nerve Surgery
Our neurosurgeons offer a wide range of procedures for the surgical management of peripheral nerve disorders, including carpal tunnel syndrome, nerve entrapment, and nerve injuries.
Minimally Invasive Procedures
At Aarogya Hastha Hospital, we strive to provide the most effective minimally invasive treatment options. Our advanced techniques allow for smaller incisions, quicker recovery, and fewer complications.

Aarogya Hastha Hospital is committed to providing all our patients with the highest standard of neurosurgical care. We believe in a comprehensive approach to treatment, considering each patient's individual needs. With our advanced services and experienced team, we aim to improve the quality of life for those suffering from neurological disorders.
