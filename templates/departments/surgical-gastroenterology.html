Surgical Gastroenterology

Expert Hands, Confident Outcomes

Gastroenterology speciality provides care for disorders of the digestive system. Our team of skilled surgeons, state-of-the-art facilities and advanced technology make us a trusted choice for patients seeking surgical solutions for their gastrointestinal issues.

The Surgical Gastroenterology department at Aarogya Hastha offers various services for gastrointestinal disorders. Here are some of the services we offer:

Our Services

Endoscopy

We have a dedicated endoscopy unit for procedures such as gastroscopy, colonoscopy, ERCP and bronchoscopy. Our team of experienced endoscopists uses the latest equipment and techniques to diagnose and treat various conditions accurately.

Laparoscopic Surgery

We specialise in laparoscopic (keyhole) surgery for a variety of conditions, such as gallbladder stones, hernias, appendicitis and many more. This minimally invasive technique offers numerous benefits to patients, such as shorter recovery time, minimal scarring and less post-operative pain.

Upper GI Surgeries

Our team of surgeons are skilled in performing a range of upper gastrointestinal surgeries, including gastric bypass, Gastroesophageal Reflux Disease (GERD) surgery and pancreatic surgery. We use advanced technology and techniques to provide safe and effective treatment for our patients.

Colorectal Surgeries

Our department offers a comprehensive range of colorectal surgeries, including colectomy, rectal resection and hemorrhoidectomy. Our surgeons are experienced in treating conditions such as inflammatory bowel disease and other disorders of the colon and rectum.

Liver Surgery

We have a team of highly skilled hepatobiliary surgeons who specialise in treating complex liver diseases. Our state-of-the-art facilities and advanced surgical techniques allow us to provide the best outcomes for patients undergoing liver surgery.

Pancreatic Surgery

Our department offers the latest in surgical techniques for treating diseases of the pancreas, such as pancreatitis, cysts and tumours. Our expert surgeons and advanced medical techniques ensures a faster recovery and better outcomes for our patients.
