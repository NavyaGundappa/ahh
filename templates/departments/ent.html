Ear, Nose & Throat (ENT)

Expert Care for All Things ENT

Department of Ear, Nose and Throat (ENT) is equipped with the latest technology to diagnose and treat major conditions. From common issues like sinusitis and ear infections to complex disorders such as hearing loss and head and neck tumours, we offer a full spectrum of services to cater to your specific needs.

Some of the common services we offer at Aarogya Hastha Hospital include:

Diagnostic evaluation
Our ENT specialists use advanced diagnostic tools, such as endoscopes and imaging techniques, to accurately diagnose conditions of the ear, nose, and throat. This helps us to design an effective treatment plan for each individual patient.
Allergy testing and treatment
If you suffer from allergies that affect your ears, nose, or throat, our allergy testing and treatment services can provide relief. We use skin prick tests and blood tests to verify the allergens that trigger your symptoms and then create a personalized treatment plan, which may include allergy shots, medications, or avoidance techniques.
Hearing and balance disorders
Our ENT specialists are trained to diagnose and treat various hearing and balance disorders, including hearing loss, tinnitus (ringing in the ears), and vertigo. We offer comprehensive hearing tests and provide hearing aids and other devices to improve your hearing and balance.
Sinus and nasal disorders
Sinusitis, nasal polyps, and deviated septum are some of the common conditions affecting the nasal passages and sinuses. Our team of experts offers medical and surgical treatments to alleviate symptoms and improve your overall quality of life.
Throat and voice disorders
Difficulty in swallowing, chronic cough, and hoarseness are some of the symptoms of throat and voice disorders, which can significantly impact your daily life. Our ENT specialists use the latest techniques and technology to diagnose and treat conditions of the throat and voice box.

Aarogya Hastha also offers a variety of surgical procedures, including ear tube insertion, tonsillectomy and sinus surgery.
