General Surgery

Surgical excellence for optimal outcomes

Welcome to the General Surgery department at Aarogya Hastha Hospital. As a modern healthcare facility, we strive to provide our patients with comprehensive and advanced surgical services. Our team of highly skilled surgeons, state-of-the-art technology, and patient-centric approach make us a trusted choice for general surgery needs.

At Aarogya Hastha Hospital, we understand that the thought of surgery can be daunting for many individuals. That's why our dedicated team addresses all your concerns and ensures a comfortable experience. Our team comprises experienced surgeons, nurses, anesthesiologists, and other support staff who work in synergy to provide the best possible care to our patients.

AH offers a wide range of general surgical procedures to cater to various medical conditions.

Some of our key services include:

Abdominal Surgery
We specialize in abdominal surgeries, including laparoscopic and open surgeries. Our surgeons are well-versed in performing procedures like appendectomy, cholecystectomy, hernia repair, and more.
Breast Surgery
Our breast surgery department offers a wide range of procedures, including lumpectomy, mastectomy, and breast reconstruction. We also have screening facilities for breast cancer to ensure early detection and timely treatment.
Colorectal Surgery
We have a dedicated team of surgeons who specialize in treating colon, rectum, and anus diseases. Our services include surgeries for colon cancer, haemorrhoids, diverticulitis, and inflammatory bowel disease.
Endocrine Surgery
Our team of endocrine surgeons has vast experience in the diagnosis and treatment of conditions affecting endocrine glands - the thyroid, parathyroid, and adrenal glands. We also offer minimally invasive surgeries to ensure faster recovery and minimal scarring.
Thyroidectomy
At Aarogya Hastha Hospital, we have a dedicated department for thyroid surgeries. Our surgeons use the latest clinical techniques to perform thyroidectomies, and our state-of-the-art equipment ensures precise and safe surgeries.

In addition to these services, we also have a well-equipped Intensive Care Unit (ICU) and post-operative recovery ward for patients who require close monitoring after surgery.

Our hospital also has advanced diagnostic facilities, including ultrasound, X-ray, and MRI, to aid in accurate diagnosis and treatment. We also have a well-stocked pharmacy, providing all the necessary medications for our patients.

At Aarogya Hastha Hospital, we prioritize patient safety and comfort above everything else. That's why we follow stringent infection control protocols, have well-equipped operation theatres, and use disposable and sterilized equipment for all procedures.
