Physiotherapy

At Aarogya Hastha Hospitals, our Physiotherapy Department offers comprehensive care aimed at helping patients restore movement, manage pain, and improve overall physical function. Our experienced physiotherapists use state-of-the-art techniques and equipment to provide personalized care, ensuring each patient receives a treatment plan tailored to their specific needs. Dedicated to guiding you on your journey to recovery with comfort and compassion are our priorities.

Orthopedic Physiotherapy
Focuses on the rehabilitation of the musculoskeletal system, treating conditions such as fractures, sprains, arthritis, post-surgical recovery, joint pain, tendonitis, bursitis and myofascitis. The goal is to restore mobility, strength, and function.
Neurological Physiotherapy
Designed for patients with neurological conditions like stroke, multiple sclerosis, Parkinson’s disease, or spinal cord injuries. This therapy aims to enhance motor function, balance, coordination, and independence.
Cardiopulmonary Physiotherapy
Supports patients with heart and lung conditions, including post-cardiac surgery or chronic obstructive pulmonary disease (COPD). It helps improve cardiovascular endurance, breathing efficiency, and overall fitness.
Sports Physiotherapy
Tailored for athletes and sports enthusiasts, this type of physiotherapy focuses on injury prevention, rehabilitation, and performance enhancement. It helps treat sports injuries such as sprains, strains, and ligament tears.
Pediatric Physiotherapy
Specialized care for children with developmental delays, cerebral palsy, or musculoskeletal conditions. The focus is on improving motor skills, posture, coordination, and strength while supporting growth and development.
Geriatric Physiotherapy
This physiotherapy addresses the unique needs of elderly patients, helping them manage conditions related to ageing, such as arthritis, osteoporosis, and mobility issues. It focuses on improving balance, strength, and functional independence.
Vestibular Rehabilitation
Focused on treating dizziness, vertigo, and balance disorders by retraining the body’s vestibular system to improve balance and reduce symptoms of dizziness.
Women’s Health Physiotherapy
Targets issues related to women’s health, such as pregnancy-related discomfort, postnatal recovery, pelvic floor dysfunction, and incontinence, helping women improve their strength and well-being.
Post-Surgical Physiotherapy
This therapy aids recovery after surgeries like joint replacements and ligament repairs by enhancing strength and mobility while minimizing post-operative complications.
Home physiotherapy
Home-based physiotherapy programs will provide regular visits by the physiotherapist which helps in a steady observation and follow-up ensuring the progression of healthcare and improvement in the condition of the patient.

At Aarogya Hastha Hospitals, our physiotherapy team is committed to delivering the highest standard of care, helping patients to regain function and live healthier, active lives. Whether recovering from surgery, managing a chronic condition, or overcoming an injury, our tailored programs are designed to meet your specific rehabilitation needs.
