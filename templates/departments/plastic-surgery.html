  • Botox and Filler Injections

Reconstructive Surgery

Reconstructive surgery aims to restore or improve the function and appearance of a part of the body that has been affected by injury, congenital disabilities or previous surgery. Our reconstructive surgery services include:

  • Breast Reconstruction
  • Scar Revision
  • Cleft Lip and Palate Repair
  • Burn Scar Reconstruction
  • Hand Surgery
