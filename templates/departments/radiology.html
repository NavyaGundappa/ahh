The Radiology Department at Aarogya Hastha is always ready to provide comprehensive and advanced imaging services to aid in the detection, diagnosis, and treatment of various medical conditions. Our state-of-the-art equipment and highly trained staff ensure accurate and timely results for all our patients.

Our Services

Digital X-Ray

Our advanced digital X-ray technology produces high-quality images with minimal radiation exposure, making it safe for patients of all ages.

C-arm Procedures

Our C-arm machine allows for real-time imaging during surgeries and other procedures, providing precise guidance for the best outcomes.

Ultrasound

We offer fully digitalized ultrasound facilities with high-resolution imaging for various organs and systems in the body.

Ultrasound Guided Procedures

We perform minimally invasive procedures such as biopsies, drainages, and injections under ultrasound guidance, ensuring accuracy and safety.

Ultrasound Colour Doppler Study

This procedure uses sound waves to create color images of blood vessels, aiding in the assessment of blood flow and identifying any abnormalities.

3D/4D Scan

Our advanced ultrasound technology allows for the visualization of the fetus in three or four dimensions, providing parents with a clear image of their baby.

CT Scan 96 Slice

Our 96-slice CT scanner produces highly detailed images, allowing for better visualization of various organs and structures in the body.

Obstetric Imaging

Our obstetric imaging services include NT scan, anomaly scan, growth scan with BPP, and Doppler, allowing for the monitoring of fetal growth and development during pregnancy.

Female Imaging

We provide specialized services for gynecological and breast ultrasound, ensuring comprehensive care for women's health.

Vascular Imaging for Extremities

Our state-of-the-art vascular imaging technology helps in the diagnosis and treatment of various circulatory system disorders in the extremities.

Foetal Scans

We offer a range of specialized fetal scans, including NT scan, Anomaly scan, Growth scan with BPP, and Doppler, providing a comprehensive assessment of fetal health.

General USG

Our ultrasound services also cover abdominal and pelvic scans, soft tissue scans, sonomammography, thyroid scans, and vascular dopplers.

HSG Procedure

Our HSG procedure uses X-ray and contrast dye to evaluate the female reproductive system, aiding in the diagnosis and treatment of various conditions.

Digital X-Ray

Our advanced digital X-ray technology produces high-quality images with minimal radiation exposure, making it safe for patients of all ages.

Our team of experienced radiologists and technicians work together to provide accurate and timely results, helping physicians make informed decisions for their patients. At Aarogya Hastha, we strive to provide the best care for all our patients.
