Urology

Welcome to the Urology department at Aarogya Hastha Hospital. We take pride in providing our patients with comprehensive and compassionate care for all their urologic needs. Our experienced and skilled urologists work tirelessly to ensure that our patients receive the best possible treatment and care.

At Aarogya Hastha Hospital, we offer a wide range of urological services to cater to the diverse needs of our patients. We offer clinically advanced techniques in treating major urological conditions including urinary tract infections, prostate problems, kidney stones, infertility, and other disorders of the urinary tract and reproductive system.

Some of the services offered by our Urology department include:

Diagnostic Tests
Our hospital is equipped with state-of-the-art diagnostic tools and techniques to diagnose urological conditions accurately. We offer a comprehensive range of tests such as urine analysis, ultrasound, prostate-specific antigen (PSA) test, and more.
Endoscopic Procedures
We offer minimally invasive endoscopic procedures that treat conditions such as kidney stones, bladder tumours, and urethral strictures. These procedures ensure faster recovery and minimal scarring.
Laparoscopic Surgery
Our team of urologists is well-versed in performing laparoscopic surgery for conditions such as kidney cancer, ureteric strictures, and bladder diverticula. This type of surgery offers many advantages, including smaller incisions, less scarring, and faster recovery.
Kidney Transplantation
Aarogya Hastha Hospital is a renowned centre for kidney transplantation. Our urologists work in close collaboration with the transplant team to provide end-to-end care to patients with kidney disorders who require a transplant.
Andrology Clinic
Our Andrology Clinic is dedicated to diagnosing and treating male sexual and reproductive health issues. Our team offers specialized treatments such as erectile dysfunction management, vasectomy, and vasectomy reversal.

Our Urology department at Aarogya Hastha Hospital offers comprehensive and specialized care to patients with urological problems. Our team of experts, along with the latest technology and equipment, ensure the best possible outcomes for our patients. We are committed to providing you with the best possible care and helping you achieve good urologic health.
