Dermatology

Healthy skin, inside and out

The dermatology department at Aarogya Hastha Hospital can provide the best possible care for your skin, hair and nail wellness. Our team of experienced dermatologists and certified technicians are committed in keeping your dermatological health at its best. With a comprehensive range of services, we address all your skin concerns to help you achieve a healthy and glowing skin.

Aarogya Hastha offers a variety of services and treatments to cater to all your skin, hair and nail concerns. Some of the services available at our dermatology department are:

Acne Treatment
Acne is a common skin condition that affects people of all ages. Our dermatologists take a personalized approach to treat acne based on the severity and type of acne. We offer various treatments like topical medication, oral medications, chemical peels, and laser therapies to help you achieve clear and blemish-free skin.
Skin Allergy Treatment
Skin allergies can cause physical and emotional discomfort and affect the quality of your life. Our dermatologists specialize in identifying the cause of your skin allergy and provide treatments like medications, topical creams, and immunotherapy to help you get relief.
Hair and Scalp Treatment
Hair fall and scalp conditions can be distressing for many individuals. Our dermatologists offer a range of treatments for hair regrowth, dandruff, scalp psoriasis, and other hair and scalp-related issues. These treatments include PRP therapy, mesotherapy, hair transplant, and scalp micro-pigmentation.
Laser Treatments
We use advanced laser technology for multiple skin concerns like hair removal, acne scars, pigmentation, and skin rejuvenation. Our laser treatments are safe, effective, and customized to suit your skin type, giving you long-lasting results.
Botox and Fillers
Aging signs like wrinkles, fine lines, and sagging skin can be effectively treated with Botox and fillers. Our experienced dermatologists will help you achieve a youthful and natural-looking appearance with minimal downtime.
Dermatologic Surgeries
Our dermatologists are skilled in performing various dermatologic surgeries, including mole removal, skin biopsy, cyst removal, and scar revision. Our specialists perform procedures with precision and care to ensure minimal scarring.

We understand the importance of maintaining good skin health, and at Aarogya Hastha Hospital, we also offer preventive care and skin maintenance treatments. Our dermatologists will guide you on the best skincare routine, which includes regular facials, chemical peels, and other skin rejuvenation treatments.
