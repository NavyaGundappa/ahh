Minimal Access Surgery

Less invasive, faster recovery

At Aarogya Hastha, we understand that facing any surgery can be a daunting experience. Our dedication to making your journey towards faster recovery is our priority. We offer the highest quality of care through minimally invasive techniques to treat various medical conditions.

Our Services

Laparoscopic Hysterectomy

The procedure is used to remove the uterus through small incisions in the abdomen. It offers a quicker recovery time, less scarring and reduced blood loss compared to traditional open surgery.

Laparoscopic Cholecystectomy

Also known as gallbladder removal surgery, this technique involves removing the gallbladder through small incisions instead of a large cut in the abdomen. It is the preferred method for treating gallbladder diseases and offers a shorter recovery time.

Laparoscopic Appendectomy

The procedure is a minimally invasive surgery to remove the appendix, which is a small organ located in the lower right side of the abdomen. This technique results in smaller incisions, less post-operative pain and a faster recovery time.

Laparoscopic Hernia Repair

The procedure is used to treat a hernia. It is a bulge in the abdominal wall caused by a weakened spot in the muscle or tissue. Laparoscopic Hernia Repair offers lower recurrence rates and is painless to allow a quicker return to normal activities.

Laparoscopic Nephrectomy

The procedure is a minimally invasive technique used to remove a diseased or damaged kidney through small incisions in the abdomen. Laparoscopic Nephrectomy benefits patients with less post-operative pain, reduced blood loss and thus offering a faster recovery time.
